# Legacy Archive

This directory preserves files that are not part of the active SORKIN-2
working surface.

The files here are not deleted. They are kept for provenance, inspection, and
possible future audit. Many of them are exploratory, derived, or draft
artifacts from branches that are not being followed in the current SORKIN-2
stage.

Do not cite material in this archive as a scientific result without a fresh
audit of its inputs, provenance, interpretation, and relation to known-truth
recoverability.

Current archive layout:

- `exploratory_phases/`: Phase1E, Phase2G, Phase3, Phase4A/4B, and Phase5
  exploratory benchmark artifacts, scripts, and tests.
- `outputs/`: PySR and related exploratory output directories.
- `run_logs/`: local run logs retained for provenance.
- `papers_and_prompts/`: exploratory notes, prompts, images, and draft papers.

The active SORKIN-2 work remains focused on algorithmic recoverability in the
Bombelli annealer: known-truth cases, zero-energy accessibility, basin
accessibility, schedule sensitivity, and historical energy/move-set
limitations.
